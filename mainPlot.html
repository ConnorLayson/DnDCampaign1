Session One

Tanner & Dylan are roommates on an earth-like Planet in a boring as fuck town. Nothing ever happens, until they wake up to the whole house shaking. As they look outside, the local city malitia is marching toward their house to tear it down. As they go outside, their friends (Whom they don't have the faintest idea are from another planet), Laz, Zeph, & Jose, come around the corner and are trying to tell them [Tanner and Dylan] something important: A fleet of Jargon constructor ships are in-route to destroy the entire planet! The aliens are attempting to convince Tanner & Dylan that their house doesn't matter in the grand scheme of it all. The rest of the session should be the party introducing themselves and hitchhiking off the planet using a patent-pending hitchhiking device that transmats them to the Jargon constructor fleet.
